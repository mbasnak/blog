Ingredients

  • 2 small to medium potatoes

  • 1 big leek

  • 1 onion

  • 2 garlic cloves

  • 1 vegetable bouillon cube

  • 1/4 cup grated cheese

  • salt to taste

  • pepper to taste

  • olive oil to taste

Directions

  • Mince the garlic and onion. Saute in olive oil with salt and pepper for 2 min.

  • Chop the leek. Save some of the green part at the end to use later, and add the rest to the onion. cook for about 5 more min.

  • Add the potatoes cut in small cubes, the bouillon cube, and cover with water.

  • Cook until the potatoes are soft and turn off the stove.

  • Add the grated cheese and, if necessary, more seasoning.

  • Chop the remaining leek very thinly, and saute in olive oil.

  • Serve the soup as it is or blend it to turn it into a creamy soup. Serve with the sauteed leek on top.
