Ingredients

  • 1 butternut squash

  • 1 onion

  • 1 tsp curry

  • 1/2 cup red lentils

  • 1/4 vegetable bouillon cube

  • 2 tbsp spicy tomato sauce

  • 2 big cremini mushrooms

  • 1 handfull spinach

  • salt to taste

  • pepper to taste

  • olive oil to taste

  • parsley and cilantro to taste

Directions

  • To roast the squash, cut it in two, remove the seeds, place both halves with some salt face down on an oven dish with some olive oil, and cook at medium-high heat for about 1 h.

  • To make this curried lentils filling, saute 1 chopped onion in olive oil for about 3 min.

  • Add 1 tsp curry and cook 1 more min.

  • Add the lentils.

  • Add the bouillon cube and the spicy tomato sauce and cover with water. Cook for 5 min.

  • Add the sliced mushrooms and the spinach and cook until the lentils are done.

  • When the squash is soft, remove some of the pulp, mix it with the filling, stuff the squash and add chopped parsley and cilantro.
