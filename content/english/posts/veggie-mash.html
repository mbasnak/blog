You can make mash with almost any veggie, so start trying because they are delcious! Here are my recipes for carrot and eggplant mash.

Ingredients

Carrot mash

  • 8 medium carrots

  • 1 onion

  • 2 tbsp honey

  • 1/3 cup milk

  • rosemary to taste

  • salt to taste

  • pepper to taste

  • olive oil to taste

Eggplant mash

  • 1 big eggplant

  • 1 garlic clove

  • 1 tsp oregano

  • 1/2 tsp cumin

  • salt to taste

  • pepper to taste

  • olive oil to taste

  • a splash of lemon juice

Directions

Carrot mash

  • Cut the carrots and put them in an oven dish with the chopped onion, 2 tbsp honey, salt, pepper, rosemary and olive oil.

  • Cook at medium-high heat for about 40 min.

  • Blend it with the milk and if necessary, add more seasoning.

Eggplant mash

  • Cut one eggplant in two, add some salt, and put both halves face down in an oven dish with some olive oil.

  • Cook at medum-high heat for about 40 min.

  • Remove the pulp from the eggplants, add 1 minced garlic clove, 1 tsp oregano, 1/2 tsp cumin, salt, pepper and a splash of lemon juice.

  • This is also delicious on a piece of toast!
