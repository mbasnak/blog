Who doesn’t love cheese? This recipe is pure cheese and super easy if you want to try a different snack!

Ingredients

  • Your favorite cheese (I used mozzarella), grated

  • Add ons like seeds, pieces of ham, pieces of caramelized onion,…

Directions

  • Make small mounts of your favorite grated cheese on an oven dish with parchment paper (or aluminum foil with some olive oil) and cook at medium heat for 6-7 min.

  • Before you cook them, you can seems, ham or any other thing you like and you will get delicious cheese crisps as a snack.
