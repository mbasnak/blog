Ingredients (For 2 people who want to eat a lot or for 3)

  • 1.65 pounds sweet potato

  • 1 egg yolk

  • 1 and 2/3 cups of all purpose flour

  • a little bit of mozzarella cheese for the stuffing

  • 1 tbsp butter

  • salt to taste

  • pepper to taste

  • nutmeg to taste

Directions

  • Boil the sweet potatoes and make a mash with the tbsp of butter.

  • Season with salt, pepper and nutmeg.

  • Add the egg yolk and flour and combine until you obtain a homogeneous dough.

  • Shape the gnocci, bigger than regular gnocci, and add a little bit of cheese inside.

  • Cook in boiling water and when they rise in the pot, strain.

  • Serve with your favorite sauce. I chose to serve it with bechamel sauce (made with 1 tbsp butter, 1 and 1/2 tbsp all purpose flour, and 1 cup of milk), spinach and mushrooms sauted with garlic, and grated cheese.
