--- title: "Garlic naan" date: "2020-04-30 08:56:59" image: images/blog/garlic-naan.png ---

Enjoy this very quick and easy garlic naan version! (or plain naan if you prefer it). This recipe yields 4 thicker naans or 6 thin naans.

Ingredients

  • 1 & 1/2 cup all purpose flour

  • 2 tsp sugar

  • 1 tbsp oil

  • 1/4 to 1/3 cups milk

  • 1/2 tsp baking powder

  • 1/2 tsp salt

  • 2 garlic cloves

  • 2 tbsp butter

Directions

  • Mix the flour, baking powder and salt.

  • Make a well in the middle of the mix and add the oil and milk.

  • Knead until you get a homogeneous roll, adding flour if necessary.

  • Leave the dough rest for 10 min.

  • Divide the dough and roll to get the desired width.

  • Cook the bread in a very hot pan with a splash of oil, 1 min on each side or until it gets golden.

  • Mince the garlic, mix with the butter and heat in the microwave until the butter melts.

  • Serve the bread with the garlic-butter mix (I like it with raw garlic, but you can saute the garlic first if you prefer it that way).
