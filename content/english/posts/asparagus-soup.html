--- title: "Asparagus soup" date: "2020-04-07 12:56:21" image: images/blog/sopa-de-esparragos.png tags: ["vegetarian"] ---

You can replace the asparagus with broccoli and it will also be very tasty :)

Ingredients

  • 0.75 pounds asparagus

  • 1 vegetable bouillon cube

  • 1 onion

  • 2 garlic cloves

  • 1/2 lemon

  • a splash of heavy cream

  • salt to taste

  • pepper to taste

  • olive oil to taste

  • grated cheese to taste

Directions

  • Chop the garlic and onion. Saute in olive oil for a few min. Add salt and pepper.

  • Add the asparagus chopped in pieces and saute a few more min.

  • Add the bouillon cube and cover with water. Cook for about 10 min or until the asparagus can be easily pierced with a fork.

  • Remove from the stove and blend.

  • Add a splash of heavy crem, the juice of half a lemon and some of grated cheese.
