--- title: "Shakshouka" date: "2020-04-30 09:23:30" image: images/blog/shakshouka.png ---

Ingredients

  • 1 onion

  • 1/2 red bell pepper

  • 2 tomatoes

  • olive oil to taste

  • salt to taste

  • pepper to taste

  • 2 garlic cloves

  • 2 tsp paprika

  • crushed red pepper to taste

  • 1 tsp cumin

  • 1 can cubed tomato

  • 3 eggs

  • cilantro to taste

  • parsley to taste

Directions

  • Saute the chopped onion, red bell pepper and tomatoes in olive oil with salt and pepper. Cook for about 15 min.

  • Add the minced garlic, paprika, some crushed red pepper and cumin. Cook 1 more min.

  • Add the can of cubed tomato. Cook 5-10 more min.

  • Add 3 raw eggs, cover the pot or pan and cook until the egg white is white (about 4 min).

  • Serve with chopped cilantro and parsley.
