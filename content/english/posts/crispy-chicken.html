--- title: "Crispy chicken with oats" date: "2020-05-09 10:38:55" image: images/blog/pollo-crocante.png ---

Make this crispy chicken without breadcrumbs or egg!

Ingredients

  • 2 chicken breasts

  • 3 tbsp mustard

  • 1 and 1/2 tsp garlic powder or minced garlic

  • 2 tbsp olive oil

  • 1/2 cup oats

  • 1 and 1/2 tsp paprika

  • salt to taste

  • pepper to taste

Directions

  • Make a paste mixing the mustard, garlic, olive oil, salt and pepper.

  • Cover the chicken breasts (cut in smaller pieces if they’re too big) with the paste.

  • Grind the oats and the paprika.

  • Roll the chicken breasts on the oat mix.

  • Cook at medium heat in the oven until they’re very golden (you can fry them if you prefer).
