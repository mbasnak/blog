Ingredients

  • 1 cup of red lentils

  • 1 can of tomato sauce

  • 1 onion

  • 3 garlic cloves

  • 1 carrot

  • 1/2 vegetable bouillon cube

  • 2 cups of water

  • 1/4 lemon

  • olive oil to taste

  • salt to taste

  • pepper to taste

  • red pepper flakes to taste

  • paprika to taste

  • cumin to taste

Directions

  • Heat some olive oil in a pot.

  • Add the finely chopped garlic and onion. Saute for about 3 min.

  • Add the carrot chopped in small cubes, the lentils and the tomato sauce.

  • Add water and the bouillon cube.

  • Season with salt, pepper, red pepper flakes, paprika and cumin to taste.

  • Cover and cook in a medium-low heat for about 20 min.

  • Take half or 2/3 of the soup and blend until itâ€™s smooth.

  • Mix both halfs of soup, adjust the seasoning and add some lemon juice.
