These stuffed mushrooms are great as an appetizer for a party, or as a side dish for lunch/dinner!

Ingredients

  • 0.6 pounds of mushrooms that can be stuffed (cremini, portobello,â€¦)

  • 1 onion

  • 2 garlic cloves

  • 2 spring onions

  • 3 tbsp cream cheese

  • 1/2 lemon

  • 2 tbsp breadcrumbs

  • salt to taste

  • pepper to taste

  • thyme to taste

  • olive oil to taste

Directions

  • Remove the base from the mushrooms and chop very finely.

  • Chop the onion and garlic aud saute in olive oil for about 3 min adding salt and pepper.

  • Add the chopped spring onion and the mushroom bases and cook for 2 more min.

  • Remove from the stove and mix with cream cheese, breadcrumbs, juice from half a lemon and thyme. If necessary, add more salt and pepper.

  • Stuff the mushrooms and cook at medium heat for about 20 min.
