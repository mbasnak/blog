--- title: "Chickpea curry" date: "2020-03-28 17:13:39" image: images/blog/CurryDeGarbanzos.png tags: ["vegetarian"] ---

Ingredients

  • 1 chickpea can

  • 1 can of tomato sauce

  • 200 gr of frozen sweet peas

  • 1/2 red pepper

  • 1/2 green pepper

  • 1 onion

  • 3 garlic cloves

  • 1 tsp garam masala

  • 1 tsp turmeric

  • 1 tsp cumin

  • 1/2 tsp paprika

  • salt to taste

  • pepper to taste

  • heavy cream to taste

  • olive oil to taste

Directions

  • Heat olive oil in a pot.

  • Add the minced garic cloves and saute for a minute.

  • Add the chopped onion and peppers and cook for about 5 min.

  • Add the garam masala, turmeric, cumin, and paprika. Cook for 2 minutes.

  • Strain the chickpeas and add to the pot.

  • Add the tomato sauce, salt and pepper. Cover and cook for about 15 min.

  • Add the frozen peas and a splash of cream and cook for 10 more min.

  • Serve with rice or quinoa.
