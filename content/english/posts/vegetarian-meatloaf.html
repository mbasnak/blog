--- title: "Vegetarian 'meatloaf'" date: "2020-04-05 15:34:12" image: images/blog/pan-de-carne-vegetariano.png tags: ["vegetarian"] ---

Ingredients

  • 1 can of black beans

  • 1 carrot

  • 2 onions

  • 5 garlic cloves

  • 2/3 cup breadcrumbs

  • 1 egg

  • 1 can tomato sauce

  • oregano to taste

  • crushed red pepper to taste

  • cumin to taste

  • salt to taste

  • pepper to taste

  • olive oil to taste

Directions

  • Strain the can of black beans and pat dry with a paper towel.

  • Crush the beans with a fork or process them coarsely.

  • Chop finely or process 2 garlic coves, 1 onion and the carrot.

  • Mix the beans with the onion mix. Add salt, pepper, cumin and crushed red pepper.

  • Add the egg and breadcrumbs and combine everything.

  • Shape the meatloaf and cook in the oven at medium heat in a dish with some olive oil.

  • While the loaf is cooking, chop the remaining onion and 2 garlic cloves.

  • Saute in a pan in olive oil for a few min.

  • Add the tomato sauce, salt, pepper and oregano and cook for 5-10 min.

  • Remove the loaf from the oven, flip, add the tomato sauce on top of it and go back to the oven for an extra 10 min.
