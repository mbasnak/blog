Ingredients

  • 4 carrots

  • 1 tbsp butter

  • 2 tbsp honey

  • 4 garlic cloves

  • 1/2 lemon

  • 4 tbsp olive oil

  • rosemary to taste

  • salt to taste

  • pepper to taste

Directions

  • Cut the carrots in big pieces.

  • Melt the butter in a pan and add the minced garlic, the honey, the juice of half a lemon, the olive oil and the seasoning. Heat for a few min.

  • Turn off the stove, add the carrots and combine everything.

  • Cook at medium heat in a pre-heated oven for 30 min.

  • You can eat them as an appetizer or a side dish. I served them with chickpea burgers.
