--- title: "How to roast peppers?" date: "2020-03-28 17:08:12" image: images/blog/MorronAsado.png ---

  • This technique to roast peppers is super easy!

  • You only need a pepper and a stove (it has to be a gas stove).

  • Turn on the stove and place the pepper on the flame.

  • Every couple minutes, rotate it.

  • The skin will turn black (like in the picture).

  • It looks burned but it’s ok like this!

  • Once every side gets black, remove from the stove and rinse with cold water, rubbing it to remove the skin.

  • That’s it! Roasted pepper ready to enjoy.
