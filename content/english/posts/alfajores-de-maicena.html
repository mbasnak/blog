Ingredients

  • 1 stick unsalted butter

  • 1 cup powdered sugar

  • 2 eggs

  • 1 tsp vanilla extract

  • 1 tbsp lemon zest

  • 3/4 cup all purpose flour

  • 1 and 1/2 cup cornstarch

  • 1/2 tsp baking powder

  • 1 cup dulce de leche (if you can get it, you can make it by boiling 1 can of condensed milk for 4 hs)

  • 1/2 up dessicated coconut

Directions

  • Whisk the soft butter with the powdered sugar.

  • Add the eggs one at a time.

  • Add the vanilla extract and lemon zest.

  • In another bowl, mix the flour, baking powder and cornstarch.

  • Add the dry ingredients to the wet mix.

  • Wrap the dough and put in the fridge for 30-60 min.

  • Warm the dough with your hands and roll.

  • Cut disks of your favorite size.

  • Put the disks on a baking dish with some butter on it and cook at medium heat for 10 min.

  • To assemble the alfajores, once the cookies are cold, make a sandwich with 2 cookies and some dulce de leche in the middle. Roll the alfajor on a plate with dessicated coconut.
