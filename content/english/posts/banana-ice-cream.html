--- title: "Banana chocolate ice-cream" date: "2020-05-02 19:04:03" image: images/blog/helado-banana.png ---

Ingredients

  • 250 gr heavy cream

  • 2 eggs

  • 2.5 oz sugar

  • 1 tsp vanilla extract

  • 3 ripe bananas

  • 1 or 2 handfulls of chocolate chips or chopped chocolate

  • water to taste

Directions

  • Whisk the cream until it gets firm, but not so firm that you can turn the bowl over your head.

  • Add the ripe mashed bananas, the vanilla extract, and the chocolate.

  • In a small pot, make a siroup heating the sugar with some water (enough water to barely cover the sugar) until the sugar disolves and the liquid starts making medium size bubbles.

  • While the siroup is heating, whisk the eggs (ideally with a machine) until they make a thick and foamy mix.

  • Pour the hot siroup gradually over the eggs and keep whisking the mix util the bowl is cold.

  • Add this mix to the banana cream mix.

  • Freeze for at least 7 h.
