Ingredients

For the pastry

  • 2 ounces vanilla biscuits

  • 1/3 stick butter

  • 2 tbsp grated coconut

For the filling

  • 1 and 1/2 lemon

  • 1/4 cup sugar

  • 1 egg

  • 1/2 stick butter

Directions

  • Process the biscuits with the butter and coconut.

  • Cover the base and walls of the tins with the mix, pressing with a spoon.

  • Put the tins in the freezer for 10 min, while you preheat the oven to medium heat.

  • Cook for about 5 min.

  • For the filling, in a bowl in bain marie whisk the egg, sugar, juice of the lemon and a half, and zest of half a lemon.

  • Add the butter in small cubes and stir constantly.

  • Once the mix thickens (about 5 min), turn off the stove.

  • Cover the base of the tartelets with the lemon mix and put in the fridge until cold and set.

  • To decorate them, you can add some powedered sugar on top, caramelized lemon peel o some of the coconut crumble we used for the base (if you add the crumble, cook in the oven for an extra 2 min for it to get golden).
