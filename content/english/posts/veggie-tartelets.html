Ingredients

For the pastry

  • 2 cups all purpose flour

  • 1 tsp salt

  • 2 tsp thyme

  • 1/2 cup water

  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil

For the filling

  • 1 onion

  • 1 red bell pepper

  • 1/2 small broccoli head

  • 1 zucchini

  • 2 big cremini mushrooms

  • 2 handfulls grated mozzarella

  • salt to taste

  • pepper to taste

  • olive oil to taste

For the binding mix

  • 3 eggs

  • 2 tbsp heavy cream

  • salt to taste

  • pepper to taste

Directions

  • For the pastry, combine all the ingredients.

  • Knead until you get a homogeneous dough and leave it aside.

  • For the filling, cut the onion and red pepper and saute in olive oil a few min.

  • Add the chopped broccoli and cook a few more min.

  • Add the chopped zucchini and sliced mushrooms. Season with salt and pepper and cook until the broccoli is done.

  • Roll the dough. You can make 1 individual big quiche or 4 small ones. I didnâ€™t have a tin for making small tartlets, so I shaped the pastry in a way that would contain the filling.

  • Fill the tartlets with the veggies, add the grated mozzarella and a few tbsp of the binding mix.

  • Cook at medium heat for about 30 min.
