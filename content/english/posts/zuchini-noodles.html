These veggie noodles are delicious warm like tis but can also be enjoyed as a salad!

Ingredients

  • 4 zucchinis

  • 2 carrots

  • 4 garlic cloves

  • 1 piece of ginger

  • 2 spring onions

  • 2 tbsp almond butter (or peanut butter)

  • 4 tbsp soy sauce

  • olive oil to taste

  • salt to taste

  • pepper to taste

  • seeds to taste

Directions

  • Make zucchini and carrot noodles with a potato peeler.

  • Chop 2 garlic cloves, 2 spring onions and a piece of ginger. Saute in olive oil with salt and pepper for 2 min.

  • Add the veggie thins, the almond butter and the soy sauce, miz very well and cook for about 5 min.

  • In a pan, saute in olive oil the 2 remaining garlic cloves and some more ginger chopped finely, as well as the seeds of your preference.

  • Serve the noodles and add the mix of garlic on top.
