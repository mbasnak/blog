Ingredients

  • 1/2 head cauliflower

  • 2 tsp turmeric

  • 2 tsp cumin

  • 2 tsp garam masala

  • salt to taste

  • pepper to taste

  • 2 tbsp olive oil + some more to saute

  • 2 garlic cloves

  • 1/2 onion

  • 1/2 red bell pepper

  • 2 cm ginger

  • 2/3 can tomato sauce

  • 1 cup heavy cream

Directions

  • Cut the cauliflower in florets.

  • Mix with 1 tsp turmeric, 1 tsp paprika, 1 tsp garam masala, salt, pepper and 2 tbsp olive oil.

  • Put in an oven dish and cook at high heat for about 25 min.

  • In the meantime, in a pot with olive oil, saute the chopped garlic, onion, red pepper and ginger, with salt and pepper, for about 3 min.

  • Add the same seasoning you added to the cauliflower, in the same amounts, y cook 1 more min.

  • Acc the tomato sauce and heave cream and cook a few min.

  • When the cauliflower is done, pour it in the pot with sauce.

  • Serve with white rice and garlic naan.
