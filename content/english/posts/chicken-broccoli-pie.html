--- title: "Chicken and broccoli pie" date: "2020-04-03 13:19:23" image: images/blog/tarta-pollo-brocoli.png ---

Ingredients

For the dough - 2 cups of all purpose flour

  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil

  • 1/2 cup water

  • thyme to taste

  • salt to taste

For the filling - 1 chicken breast (about 0.750 pounds)

  • 0.750 pounds broccoli

  • 1 onion

  • 3 garlic cloves

  • salt to taste

  • pepper to taste

  • olive oil to taste

For the binding mix - 1/2 pound bechamel sauce

  • 2 eggs

  • 2 tbsp grated cheese

  • seeds to taste

Directions

For the dough - Mix the flour with the salt and thyme.

  • Add the oil.

  • Gradually add the water and combine.

  • Knead until you get a homogeneous and soft roll.

  • Roll and cover a pie dish.

For the filling - Saute the onion and garlic finely chopped in olive oil for a couple min.

  • Add the chicken cut in cubes, the broccoli cut in small pieces, salt and pepper.

  • Cook for about 10 min or until the chicken is done.

Assembly - Put together the binding: mix the bechame sauce with 2 eggs and 2 tbsp of grated cheese.

  • Add the filling to the base, and add the binding mix.

  • Cook at medium heat in the oven for about 50 min.
