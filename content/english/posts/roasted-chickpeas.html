These roasted chickpeas with spices are delicious in salads, soups, in rice with veggies or as a snack!

Ingredients

  • 1 can of chickpeas

  • 2 tbsp olive oil

  • 1 tsp cumin

  • 1 tsp paprika

  • 1 tsp turmeric

  • salt to taste

  • pepper to taste

Directions

  • Drain the can of chickpeas and pat them dry.

  • Add the seasoning and oil and combine everything together.

  • Cook in the oven at high heat for about 40 min or until they have a texture youâ€™re happy with.
