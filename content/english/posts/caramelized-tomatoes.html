These tomatoes are delicious for breakfast, as an appetizer or as a side dish. They are super easy to make!

Ingredients

  • 4 tomatoes

  • 4 garlic cloves

  • 1 tbsp brown sugar

  • olive oil to taste

  • salt to taste

  • pepper to taste

  • 1 tsp thyme

  • 1 tsp oregano

Directions

  • Cut the tomatoes in four pieces.

  • Chop the garlic cloves.

  • Mix all the ingredients and put the seasoned tomatoes in an oven dish.

  • Cook at high heat in the oven for about 15 min.
