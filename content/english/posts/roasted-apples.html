These roasted apples are ideal for when you’re craving something sweet but don’t have too many ingredients, or don’t feel like cooking a very elaborated dish! I served them with toasted walnuts, but they are also delicious with ice cream or whipped cream (or all of the above).

Ingredients

  • granny smith apples (1 per persona)

  • 1 tbsp honey

  • cinnamon to taste

  • a splash of lemon juice

  • walnuts to taste

Directions

  • Cut the apples in halves and remove their heart.

  • Put on a oven dish and add lemon, cinamon and honey.

  • Cook in the oven at high heat for about 20 min.

  • Add the walut pieces, and cook for 10 more min.

  • Serve with more cinnamon and honey.
