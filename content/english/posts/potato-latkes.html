--- title: "Potato latkes" date: "2020-04-25 16:21:50" image: images/blog/latkes-de-papa.jpg tags: ["vegetarian"] ---

Ingredients

  • 4 small potatoes (about 1.3 pounds)

  • 1 egg

  • 4 tbsp flour

  • salt to taste

  • pepper to taste

  • oil to taste

Directions

  • Grate the potatoes.

  • Add the egg, the flour, the seasoning and combine everything.

  • Shape disks with your hand and put them in an oven dish with aluminum foil and some oil.

  • Cook at high heat for about 10 min, flip them, and cook them 10 more min on the other side.

  • I served them with a dip made with 2 tbsp mayo, 1 tsp mustard, 1/2 minced garlic clove and some rosemary.
