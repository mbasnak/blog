We had some leftover cooked sweet potatoes and I decided to use them for this curry. It is great because they coe apart and create an awesome texture!

Ingredients

  • 1/2 red onion

  • 0.7 pounds cooked sweet potato

  • 1 can tomato sauce

  • 1 garlic clove

  • 1 cup red lentils

  • 1 tsp cumin

  • 1 tsp turmeric

  • 1 tsp garam masala

  • 1/2 cup frozen sweet peas

  • 1 inch ginger

  • 2 cups water

  • salt to taste

  • pepper to taste

  • olive oil to taste

Directions

  • Mince the garlic, ginger and onion. Saite in olive oil with salt and pepper for about 3-4 min.

  • Add 1 tsp cumin, 1 tsp turmeric and 1 tsp garam masala and cook 1 more min.

  • Add the red lentils, tomato sauce, cooked sweet potatoes and water.
