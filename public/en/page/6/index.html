About this blog

My bubbe Perla was the best cook. When you went to her place, she stuffed you with food until you couldn’t take anymore, and then she gave you a giant container to take the leftovers home. If I got there early, she gave me some small tasks to do in the kitchen, like chopping the chocolate in small pieces for a cake. When I was about 12, my parents started taking a course one a week after work and they got home late. One day I decided not to wait until they got back for my mum to make dinner and started cooking something myself instead. From then on, I became one of the two official dinner makers at home, and my love for cooking grew daily. By the time I finished high school, my love for cooking was so big that I decided to start going to cooking school as a hobbie. That year taught me many valuable things that I still remember and use. Today I achieve a goal that I’ve had for a while, which is to publish my recipes. I hope you enjoy them as much as I do!
