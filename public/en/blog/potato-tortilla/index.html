Potato tortilla

  • vegetarian

Potat tortilla is a typical spanish dish, that we have inherited in Argentina. The base of it are potatoes and egg, but there are many different versions adding various things to it. I like it very simple, but you can get creative here! I chose to serve it with salad, and aioli sauce (garlic mayo).

Ingredients

  • .. pounds potato

  • … onion

  • … eggs

  • salt to taste

  • pepper to taste

  • paprika to taste

Directions

  • Cut the potatoes. There are different versions for how to do this too. Soem people cut them in small cubes, others in irregular pieces, and some in slices. I use this last method, and cut the potatoes in thin slices.

  • Cook them in a pot with boiling water until they’re cooked, but not soft!

  • Strain them, and pat them dry with a paper towel (this is important, or the hot oil will jump all over the place later!).

  • Cut the onion. I like to cut it in semi-circles, like this:

  • Saute the onion in olive oil with salt until it is golden.

  • Mix the potatoes, onion, eggs (whisked a bit before), salt, pepper and paprika.

  • In a pan, add some vegetable oil. Wait until it’s very hot and add the potato mix.

  • Cook for about 3 min, or until the egg on the sides is cooked.

  • Very carefully, pour the hot oil from the pan into a bowl, flip the tortilla onto a plate, add the oil back to the pan, and add the tortilla on the other side.

  • Cook for about 2 more min.
