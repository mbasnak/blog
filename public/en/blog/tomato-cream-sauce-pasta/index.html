Pasta with tomato-cream sauce

  • vegetarian

This sauce is super easy, but will result in a much better pasta than a store-bought sauce! In 20 min you will be enjoying a great dish.

Ingredients

  • the pasta of your choosing

  • 1 can of tomato sauce

  • 1/2 red onion

  • 3 garlic cloves

  • 1 tomato

  • 1/2 pepper

  • 50 cc heavy cream

  • salt to taste

  • pepper to taste

  • 2 bay leaves

  • italian seasoning to taste

  • olive oil to taste

Directions

  • Chop the onion, garlic and pepper. Saute in olive oil for about 5 min.

  • Add the previously cubed tomato, the tomato sauce and the spices.

  • Cook for about 15 min. Add the cream and, if necessary, more seasoning.

  • Cook for 2-3 extra min.

  • Serve with your favorite pasta.
