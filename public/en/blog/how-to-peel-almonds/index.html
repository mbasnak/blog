How to peel almonds

  • tip

You can buy already peeled almonds but they are usually much more expensive. If you have to use many if might be worth it, but if you need a reasonable amount and have a few extra min, a recommend peeling them at home.

  • Boil some water.

  • Once it’s boiling, add the almonds and leave for 30 to 60 sec. They are going to start rising. Don’t leave them longer because they might get soggy!

  • Turn off the heat and strain them.

  • Rinse with cold water and dry with paper towels or a clean cloth.

  • One by one, pince a little bit the skin and you will see it starts to come out.

  • When you’re done peeling them, you can dry them again.

  • Ready to be used! You can bake these delicious florentines.
