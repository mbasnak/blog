Eggplants with garlic and soy sauce

  • vegan
  • vegetarian

Ingredients

  • 1 big eggplant

  • 6 garlic cloves

  • ¼ cup olive oil

  • 5 tbsp soy sauce

  • pepper to taste

  • ¼ red onion

  • 1 small piece of ginger

Directions

  • Finely chop the garlic, ginger and onion.

  • Mix with the eggplant cut in cubes.

  • Add pepper, olive oil and soy sauce and combine everything.

  • Marinate in the fridge for 2-3 hs.

  • Put the marinated eggplants in a pot, add a splash of water, cover with a lid and cook for about 20 min.

  • I chose to serve it with white rice, greens and toasted seeds.
