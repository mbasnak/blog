Granola

I had a hard time finding a comercial granola that I liked. I usually find them too sweet, not sweet enough, or I think that they don’t have enough nuts. So I decided to make my own, and I always have it at home for breakfast. It’s really tasty on its own, or with yogurt, milk and fruits.

Ingredients

  • 4 cups of oats

  • 1 and 1/2 cups of nuts (I used almonds and walnuts)

  • 1 handful of dried fruit (I used dried cranberries)

  • 1/3 cup of honey

  • 1 splash of neutral vegetable oil

Directions

  • Pre-heat the oven at 350 F.

  • Chop the nuts

  • Combine every ingredient except for the dried fruit. If the honey is solid, melt in the microwave for a few seconds before adding.

  • Distribute the mix in the biggest oven dish you have.

  • Cook in the oven for about 10-12 min.

  • Remove from the over, mix, and cook again for 10-12 min.

  • Remove it from the oven and let it dry.

  • Add the dry fruit.
